Section 438 CrPC provides for anticipatory bail when a person apprehends arrest. This sample post shows how to format content in Markdown for Eleventy.

## Key principles

- Courts consider seriousness of the offence.
- Past conduct and antecedents matter.
- Risk of tampering with evidence is relevant.

## Drafting tips

1. Clearly state facts and chronology.
2. Provide legal citations and supporting documents.
3. Propose reasonable conditions for bail.
