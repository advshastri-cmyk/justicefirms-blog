# Triple Talaq Awareness: Rights of Muslim Women in India

Triple Talaq (Instant divorce) ko **2017 Supreme Court judgement** aur **Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Marriage) Act, 2019** ne illegal aur void declare kar diya hai.

## Key Rights
- Talaq ke baad **maintenance** ka adhikar
- **Custody** of children ka matter Civil Court decide karti hai
- Woman ko **residence protection** milti hai

## Complaint Options
- Local Police / Women Cell
- Magistrate Court under 2019 Act
- National Woman Commission complaint
