<h1>Divorce Under Hindu Marriage Act, 1955</h1>

<p>
Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 ke under divorce ka matlab sirf shaadi khatam karna nahi,
balki dono parties ko ek fair aur dignified exit dena bhi hai. Broadly, divorce ke
do major raaste hain — mutual consent (aapas ki sahmati se) aur contested (jab ek
party disagree kare).
</p>

<hr>

<h2>1. Mutual Consent Divorce – Section 13B</h2>

<p>
Ye sabse practical aur comparatively less stressful tareeka mana jata hai.
</p>

<ul>
  <li>Dono spouses khud milkar decide karte hain ki unhe alag hona hai.</li>
  <li>Generally, <strong>कम से कम 1 saal</strong> ka separation dikhana padta hai.</li>
  <li>Section 13B(2) ke under traditionally 6 months ki “cooling-off” period thi,
      jise Supreme Court ne kuch cases me <em>waive</em> karne ki permission di hai
      (matlab court zarurat padne par 6 mahine ka wait hata sakta hai).</li>
  <li>Mutual consent me normally maintenance, streedhan, custody, etc. pe pehle se
      written agreement hota hai.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Process (simple overview):</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Joint petition file hoti hai (dono ke signatures ke saath).</li>
  <li>Court verify karta hai ki consent free aur voluntary hai.</li>
  <li>Cooling-off period ke baad second motion (final hearing) hoti hai.</li>
  <li>Court decree of divorce pass karta hai.</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>2. Contested Divorce – Section 13</h2>

<p>
Jab ek spouse divorce chahta hai aur doosra agree nahi karta, to <strong>contested divorce</strong>
file hota hai. Yahan ground prove karne padte hain.
</p>

<h3>Common Grounds under Section 13</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Cruelty</strong> – mental ya physical cruelty; repeated insults, violence, humiliation, etc.</li>
  <li><strong>Adultery</strong> – spouse ka kisi aur ke saath sexual relationship.</li>
  <li><strong>Desertion</strong> – bina reason aur consent ke <strong>कम से कम 2 saal</strong> ke liye chhod dena.</li>
  <li><strong>Conversion of religion</strong> – Hindu religion chhodkar kisi aur religion ko adopt kar lena.</li>
  <li><strong>Mental disorder</strong> – aisi condition jo marital life ko impossible bana de
      (court medical evidence dekhta hai).</li>
  <li><strong>Communicable venereal disease</strong> – serious sexually transmitted diseases.</li>
</ul>

<p>
Contested divorce zyada time-taking aur emotionally exhausting ho sakta hai, kyunki
evidence, witnesses, cross-examination sab involve hote hain.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>3. Courts & Jurisdiction</h2>

<p>Divorce petition kahaan file kar sakte hain?</p>

<ul>
  <li>Jahan <strong>marriage solemnize</strong hui (shaadi hui).</li>
  <li>Jahan husband–wife ka <strong>last residence together</strong> tha.</li>
  <li><strong>Wife</strong> jahan currently reside karti hai — us jagah ka Family Court bhi jurisdiction
      rakhta hai (ye wife-friendly provision hai).</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>4. Practical Tips & Strategy</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Agar possible ho to <strong>mutual consent</strong ka option explore karein — time, cost,
      stress sab kam hote hain.</li>
  <li>Financial issues (maintenance, alimony, streedhan, children’s expenses) pe
      clear written understanding rakhein.</li>
  <li>Child custody matters always <strong>“best interest of child”</strong> ke principle pe decide hote hain.</li>
  <li>Social pressure ya emotional guilt ke basis par karar mat lein —
      legally informed decision lena zaroori hai.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Note:</strong> Har case alag hota hai. Same section ke under bhi result facts pe depend karega.
Isliye kisi bhi serious step se pehle proper legal advice lena bahut zaroori hai.</p>
