Understanding Anticipatory Bail

Published on Feb 1, 2025 • Criminal Law

Advocate R. Sharma

15+ years experience in Criminal and Corporate Litigation.

Section 438 CrPC provides for anticipatory bail when a person apprehends arrest. This article explains the key principles and practical drafting tips for relief applications.

When is anticipatory bail granted?

Court factors include nature of the offense, antecedents of the accused, and possibility of tampering with evidence.

Drafting considerations

Set out facts, law, and supporting material; propose appropriate conditions for grant of bail.
